  The **Picktopia Annual Moneyball Classic** is our most prestigious tournament of the year, bringing together the best players from our community for an unforgettable competition.

qualificationProcess: |
  ## How to Qualify

  Entry to the Annual Moneyball Classic is earned through participation in our **monthly qualifying tournaments** throughout the year.

  ### Qualification Path
  - **Monthly Tournaments**: Participate in any of our 12 monthly tournaments
  - **Points System**: Earn qualification points based on your placement
  - **Top Performers**: The top 32 players by points automatically qualify
  - **Wild Cards**: 8 additional spots awarded based on overall community participation

  ### Monthly Tournament Schedule
  Tournaments are held on the last Saturday of each month from January through December. Each monthly tournament contributes to both your qualification standing and the overall prize pool.

prizePool: |
  ## Prize Pool Structure

  The Annual Moneyball Classic features one of the largest prize pools in regional pickleball, funded through our innovative community model.

  ### How the Prize Pool Grows
  - **Monthly Contributions**: A portion of each monthly tournament entry fee is allocated to the annual prize pool
  - **Community Support**: Sponsorships and community partnerships
  - **Prize Distribution**: Awarded across multiple skill divisions and placements

  ### Example Breakdown (Based on Previous Year)
  - **Champion**: 30% of total prize pool
  - **Runner-up**: 20% of total prize pool
  - **Semi-finalists**: 12.5% each
  - **Quarter-finalists**: 6.25% each

format: |
  ## Tournament Format & Seeding

  ### Format
  - **Double Elimination** bracket ensuring competitive play
  - **Multiple Divisions**: Men's Singles, Women's Singles, Men's Doubles, Women's Doubles, Mixed Doubles
  - **Skill Levels**: 3.5+, 4.0+, 4.5+, 5.0+ divisions

  ### Seeding
  Players are seeded based on their performance in monthly qualifying tournaments:
  1. **Total points accumulated** throughout the year
  2. **Head-to-head records** in monthly events
  3. **Recent form** (last 3 months weighted higher)

  ### Event Details
  - **Date**: First weekend of December annually
  - **Location**: Picktopia Main Facility (all courts)
  - **Duration**: Full weekend (Saturday & Sunday)
  - **Format**: Pool play followed by elimination rounds
