# **Picktopia Moneyball Implementation Plan**

The **Picktopia Moneyball** tournament is a weekly, tiered, competitive event designed to reward top-performing Picktopia members with immediate cash prizes and a chance to qualify for an exclusive annual club championship.

## **I. Event Structure**

### **Name: The Picktopia Moneyball Series**

### **Frequency: Weekly Tournaments (Men's, Women's, and Mixed Categories)**

### **Exclusivity: Member-Only Experience**

### **Tiers of Competition:**

#### **Tier 1: Weekly Picktopia Moneyball**

* **Levels of Play:** Divisions will be created based on a team's **DUPR-regulated average rating** to ensure balanced competition. Suggested divisions include: **4.0, 4.5, and 5.0**.  
* **Format:**  
  * **Guaranteed Play:** Participants are guaranteed a **minimum of 6 games**.  
  * **Structure:** A **randomized round robin of 5 games** will funnel into a **single elimination bracket** to determine the final rankings.  
* **Registration:** Must register with a partner for the specific weekly event.

#### **Tier 2: The Picktopia Annual Moneyball Classic**

* **Concept:** This is a high-stakes, qualifier-only tournament held **once annually** (e.g., March 2026).  
* **Qualification:** Player invitations and seeding are determined by a player's cumulative **number of wins** or top-three finishes across all weekly Picktopia Moneyball events throughout the year.  
* **Payouts:** Winnings are drawn from the accumulating **Annual Prize Pool** and paid out per division.
