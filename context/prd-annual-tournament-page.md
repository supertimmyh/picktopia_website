# Product Requirements Document: Annual Tournament Page

## 1. Introduction & Background

Picktopia hosts a premier "Annual Moneyball Classic" tournament, the culmination of a year-long series of monthly qualifying events. Currently, there is no dedicated space on the website to promote this flagship tournament, explain its significance, or detail the qualification process. This page will serve as the central hub of information for this event, driving player engagement and participation in the monthly feeder tournaments.

## 2. Goals & Objectives

*   To create a visually engaging and informative page for the "The Picktopia Annual Moneyball Classic".
*   To clearly explain the tournament format, qualification process, and prize structure.
*   To increase player participation in the monthly qualifying tournaments.
*   To establish the Annual Moneyball Classic as a prestigious and highly anticipated event in the Picktopia community.

## 3. User Stories

*   **As a competitive player**, I want to understand how to qualify for the Annual Moneyball Classic so I can plan my participation in the monthly tournaments.
*   **As a new member**, I want to learn about the biggest tournament Picktopia offers to see what I can aspire to.
*   **As a website visitor**, I want to easily find information about major Picktopia events to get a sense of the competitive scene.

## 4. Requirements & Features

*   **Content:** The page must display the following information, which will be managed via the CMS:
    *   Tournament Title: "The Picktopia Annual Moneyball Classic"
    *   Prominent hero image.
    *   A detailed description of the tournament.
    *   Clear explanation of the qualification process (via monthly tournaments).
    *   Information about the prize pool and how it's funded.
    *   Details on the tournament format and seeding.
    *   A call-to-action to encourage users to sign up for the next monthly tournament.
*   **Layout:**
    *   The page should feature a single, prominent section dedicated to the tournament, avoiding a card-based layout.
    *   It should be visually distinct and impactful to reflect the prestige of the event.
*   **Navigation:**
    *   A link to this page should be included in the main navigation, under the "Play" or a similar relevant section.
*   **CMS Integration:**
    *   The content of the page should be fully editable through the Decap CMS.

## 5. Design & UX

*   The page should have a modern and professional design, consistent with the Picktopia brand.
*   The hero section should be visually striking, with a high-quality image and a bold, clear title.
*   The content should be easy to read and scan, with clear headings and a logical flow.
*   The call-to-action should be prominent and compelling.

## 6. Assumptions & Constraints

*   The content for the page will be provided in Markdown format.
*   The page will be built using the existing React-based architecture of the Picktopia website.
*   The design should align with the existing branding and style guide.

## 7. Success Metrics

*   Increase in traffic to the monthly tournament sign-up pages.
*   Increase in the number of participants in the monthly tournaments.
*   User feedback indicating that the page is informative and easy to understand.
